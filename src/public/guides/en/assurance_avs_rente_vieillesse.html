I have a situation related to: AVS old-age pension

The ordinary retirement age for AHI is 65 years (AHI 21 law in force). A full pension requires an uninterrupted contribution period from 21 years (until 44 years for men, harmonized to 65 for women via AHI21 in 2024). Early retirement is possible 1 or 2 years years early with actuarial reduction…

Applicable law provisions

  • LAVS 21 — Age entitling to pension
  • LAVS 29 — # Ordinary Annuity
  • LAVS 40 — Anticipation and adjournment
  • LAVS 35 — Annuity for couple

Critical periods

  1. Anticipation of the file — 6 months before the legal retirement age
  2. Lodging of the Application with the Compensation Office — 3 to 4 months before the desired 1st payment (LAVS 21 / LAVS 29 / LAVS 40)
  3. Decision on Annuity — 60 to 120 days
  4. Opposition and then appeal to the cantonal insurance court — 30 days from notification (LPGA 52 / LPGA 56 / LAVS 35)
