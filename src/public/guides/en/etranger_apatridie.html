I am stateless.

Statelessness is the fact of not being recognized as a national by any State. Switzerland is a party to the Convention relating to the Status of Stateless Persons of 1954 (RS 0.142.40). Critical deadlines

  2. Application for Recognition to the SEM — in the 60 days (1954 Stateless Persons Convention / CC 30 al. 1 lit. b)
  3. Decision of the SEM — 6 to 18 months
  4. Appeal to the FAT — 30 days from notification (LTAF 31 ss / PA 50)
