I request asylum in Switzerland

The asylum procedure in Switzerland is governed by the LAsi. Since 2019, the restructured procedure provides for: (1) a preparatory phase in a federal centre (registration, Dublin examination), (2) a hearing on asylum grounds, (3) a decision.
