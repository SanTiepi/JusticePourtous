I have questions about social insurance as a foreigner

Foreigners working in Switzerland are affiliated with social insurance schemes (AVS/AI/APG/AC) like Swiss nationals, regardless of their permit (art. 1a LAVS). Critical deadlines

  1. Identification of Status and Conventions — immediate upon arrival or assumption of employment
  2. Registration with the competent funds — 3 months for LAMal, immediate for AVS/LAA via employer (LAVS 1a / LPP 25f / LAMal 3)
  3. Annual verification and updates — annually
  4. Opposition then appeal to the cantonal insurance court — 30 days from notification (LPGA 52 / LPGA 56)
