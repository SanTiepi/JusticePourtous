Critical deadlines

  1. Reception and analysis of the decision — immediate
  2. Preparation of the appeal and suspensive effect — 5 days from notification (deadline LAsi 108 para. 2) (Dublin III art. 3 / Dublin III art. 17 / LAsi 108 para. 2)
  3. Decision of the FAC — often 5 to 15 days for suspensive effect, merits longer
  4. Request for Reconsideration or Review Appeal — without delay if new facts (PA 66 / LAsi 111b)
