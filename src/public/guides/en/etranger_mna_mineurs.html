Critical deadlines

  1. Identification and reporting — immediate upon arrival (Federal Statute 17 / CDE 3)
  2. Designation of a guardian or trusted person — without delay (immediate) (Federal Act on Residence and Permanent Residence Permits for Foreign Nationals, Article 17, paragraph 3 / CC 327a)
  3. Asylum procedure and appropriate accommodation — according to asylum procedure
  4. Appeal to the FAT — 5 or 30 days from notification (ASA 108 / ATAF 31 et seq.)
