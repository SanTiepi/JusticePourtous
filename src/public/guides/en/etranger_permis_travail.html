Critical deadlines

  1. Employer File Establishment — immediate
  2. Filing of the request with the SEM via cantonal service — in the 30 days following the signature of the contract (LEI 18 / LEI 21 / LEI 22)
  3. Combined cantonal/SEM decision — 1 to 4 months according to nationality (EU/EFTA vs third country)
  4. Appeal to the cantonal court and then to the FAT — 30 days from notification (LEI 64 / PA 50)
