Critical deadlines

  1. Analysis of the notified decision — immediate upon receipt (LEI 64 / LEI 83)
  2. Consultation of a qualified representative — under 7 days
  3. Filing of the appeal with request for suspensive effect — 30 days (or notified period) (LEI 112 / FoPA 108 / PA 55)
  4. Production of evidence of integration / risks — during the proceedings (ECHR 3 / LEI 83 / FoAL 5)
  5. Decision on Appeal — according to court/authority (LTAF 31 / ECHR 34)
