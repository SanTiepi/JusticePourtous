My Schengen visa was refused

The Schengen visa of type C (short stay, max. 90 days) may be refused for the reasons provided for in the Visa Code (EU Regulation 810/2009, applicable in Switzerland). Frequent reasons: insufficient justification of the purpose of the stay, doubt regarding the intention to return, insufficient financial means,…
