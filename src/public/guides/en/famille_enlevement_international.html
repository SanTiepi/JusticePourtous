My child has been taken abroad

International child abduction is governed by the Hague Convention of 25 October 1980 (Hague Convention 80) and the Federal Act LF-EEA (RS 211.222.32). Critical Deadlines

  1. Statement of Removal — immediate
  2. Filing of the Application with the Central Authority (FCJP) — as soon as possible — deadline of 1 year to benefit from automatic return (art. 12 CLaH80) (CLaH80 art. 3 and 12 / LF-EEA 5)
  3. Procedure before the competent cantonal court — 6 weeks (CLaH80 objective)
  4. Appeal to Tribunal fédéral — 10 days from notification (urgent matter) (Art. TF 100 para. 2 lit. c)
