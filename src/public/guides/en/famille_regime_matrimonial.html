# Practical Guide

I am questioning my matrimonial regime.

The legal matrimonial regime in Switzerland is participation in acquests (art. 196-220 CC). Each spouse retains their separate property (property prior to marriage, inherited, received by donation) and acquires acquests (income from work, proceeds from separate property).
