# Practical Guide

A relative has died without a will

In the absence of a will, the succession is governed by law (intestate succession, art. 457-466 CC). The Swiss system is based on kinship lines: 1st kinship line = descendants (children, grandchildren), 2nd kinship line = father and mother and their descendants (brothers/sisters), 3rd kinship line =…

Critical Deadlines

  1. Record of Death and Inventory — in the 30 days following death
  2. Application for Certificate of Inheritance — in the 90 days (CC 559 / cantonal law)
  3. # Deadline for Repudiation — 3 months from the time of knowledge of the death (CC 567)
  4. Distribution and Closure — according to complexity (CC 602 et seq. / CC 604)
