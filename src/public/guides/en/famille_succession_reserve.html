Critical Deadlines

  1. Identification of reserved heirs — upon opening of the succession (CC 471 / CC 470)
  2. Application for Heir Certificate and Inventory — in the 6 months (CC 551 / CC 581)
  3. Analysis of donations made during lifetime — under 30 days (CC 475 / CC 626-633)
  4. Action for Reduction — 1 year as soon as aware (CC 522 / CC 533)
  5. Action relating to joint heirs — until partition (CC 626 / CC 634)
