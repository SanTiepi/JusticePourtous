I want to understand the usufruct of the spouse

The surviving spouse may request the attribution of usufruct over the children's share in place of their share in ownership (art. 473 CC). For example, if the spouse is entitled to 1/2 in ownership, they may choose usufruct over the entire estate (or over the dwelling and movable property).…
