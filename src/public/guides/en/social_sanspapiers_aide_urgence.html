I have a situation related to: Undocumented aid emergency

The Constitution guarantees every person in distress a right to minimum vital standards (art. 12 Cst), regardless of their status. Persons without status (NEM, expired permits, undocumented immigrants) are entitled to minimal emergency assistance: collective accommodation, food, urgent medical care, clothing, etc.
