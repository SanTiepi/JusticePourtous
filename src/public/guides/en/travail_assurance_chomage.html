Critical Deadlines

  1. Registration at ORP as of notice of termination — before the end of the relationship (LACI 8 / LACI 10)
  2. Filing of documents at the unemployment benefit office — under 3 months after end of employment (otherwise partial loss) (LACI 20 / OACI 29)
  3. Documented job searches — as of notice and each month (LACI 17 / OACI 26)
  4. Dispute of a suspension/refusal decision — 30 days as of decision (LA 52 / LACI 100)
  5. Appeal to the cantonal court — 30 days as from decision on objection (LPGA 56 / LPGA 61)
