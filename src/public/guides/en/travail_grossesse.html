Critical Deadlines

  1. Notice to the employer — as soon as it becomes known (CO 336c al. 1 let. c)
  2. # Adaptation of the Position — progressively according to stage (LTr 35 / OLT 1 art. 60-64)
  3. Challenge of Dismissal During Pregnancy — immediate if dismissal (CO 336c al. 1 lit. c / CO 336c al. 2)
  4. Maternity allowance APG — after childbirth, within the 5 years (LAPG 16b-16h)
