Critical Deadlines

  1. Calculation and record of salary owed — immediate (CO 322 et seq.)
  2. Written formal notice — without delay, at the latest 30 days after the due date (CO 102 / CO 323)
  3. Conciliation and/or legal proceedings — as soon as the formal notice expires (CPC 197 / LP 38 seq.)
  4. Judgment and Forced Execution — according to procedure (LP 88 et seq.)
